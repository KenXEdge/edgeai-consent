Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 14, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how XTX LLC DBA XTX-ai ("EDGEai", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the EDGEai platform and SMS messaging program. By using EDGEai, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Summary: EDGEai collects only the information needed to operate the carrier engagement platform. We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. SMS opt-in information is never shared with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information: Name, company name, email address, mobile phone number, MC number, DOT number, and subscription tier.
  • Gmail Authorization: When you connect your Gmail account, we receive an OAuth token that allows EDGEai to send and read emails on your behalf. We do not store your Gmail password. Access is limited to freight-related email processing only.
  • Broker Data: Contact information for freight brokers you add to your EDGEai account, including name, company, email, phone, and lane preferences.
  • Load and Activity Data: Records of outreach emails sent, broker responses received, load offers detected, and loads confirmed.
  • SMS Data: Your mobile phone number and SMS message history within the EDGEai platform.
  • Usage Data: Log data, IP address, device type, and platform interaction data for security and service improvement purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and deliver the EDGEai carrier engagement platform
  • To send broker outreach emails from your connected Gmail account
  • To detect and classify broker reply emails using AI analysis
  • To send SMS load alert notifications to your registered mobile number
  • To process and log load wins and broker relationship data
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • To improve platform performance and AI classification accuracy
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. SMS Messaging Program

EDGEai operates an SMS notification program that sends load alert messages to registered carrier phone numbers. The following terms apply to our SMS program:

  • Consent: You provide consent to receive SMS messages during account registration at xedge-ai.com.
  • Message Types: Load alert notifications, agent status updates, and platform notifications.
  • Message Frequency: Message frequency varies based on broker and load board activity. Typically 1–10 messages per day.
  • Message and Data Rates: Message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier plan.
  • Opt-Out: Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe. You will receive one confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.
  • Help: Reply HELP for assistance or contact support@xedge-ai.com.
  • SMS Data: Your SMS opt-in information and consent records are never shared with third parties or used for marketing purposes unrelated to EDGEai services.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use trusted third-party services to operate the platform, including Google Cloud (infrastructure), Supabase (database), Twilio (SMS delivery), and Anthropic (AI classification). These providers process data only as necessary to deliver their services and are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business Transfer: If EDGEai is acquired or merged, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission, secure database storage, OAuth-based authentication, and access controls. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and protect your account credentials.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account information and activity data for as long as your account is active. Upon account cancellation, we retain data for 90 days before deletion, except where retention is required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting support@xedge-ai.com.

7. Your Rights

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may update your account information at any time through the EDGEai dashboard.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt-Out of SMS: Reply STOP to any SMS message to unsubscribe immediately.
  • Gmail Disconnect: You may revoke EDGEai's access to your Gmail account at any time through your Google Account settings.

8. Children's Privacy

EDGEai is a business-to-business platform intended for use by licensed freight carriers and business operators. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under the age of 18.

9. Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of material changes via email. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
